About the Artists

H.E.R., born Gabriella Sarmiento Wilson on June 27, 1997, in Vallejo, California, is a multi-talented individual renowned for her contributions to the music industry. Formerly known as Gabi Wilson, she transitioned to the stage name H.E.R., an acronym for "Having Everything Revealed," embodying an enigmatic persona. Beyond her musical prowess, H.E.R. is recognized for her authenticity and empowerment in the entertainment landscape. Her journey, marked by early recognition and a deliberate air of mystery, reflects her commitment to personal expression and artistic innovation.

Temilade Openiyi, professionally known as Tems, is a Nigerian singer and songwriter born and raised in Lagos. Born on June 11, 1995, Tems has made a notable impact on the music scene with her soulful voice and distinctive sound, fusing Afrobeat, R&B, and alternative elements. Tems gained widespread recognition for her collaborations with top artists and her solo works, captivating audiences with her unique style and emotive storytelling. Her journey reflects a commitment to self-expression and artistic innovation, establishing her as a rising star in the Nigerian music industry.
